The Journey of Iconic Landmarks and Breathtaking views

How its Started

- Nishant Visited Dubai in the year 2023 during a 48-hour layover on the way to Canada for higher education.

Burj Khalifa

-First stop was the iconic Burj Khalifa, the tallest building in the world, located in Dubai, UAE. It stands at 828 meters (2,717 feet) with 163 floors

Impressed by the rapid ascent to the 124th floor in just one minute, symbolizing international cooperation and the progress of the Middle East. The Burj Khalifa is known for its dynamic lighting displays and is an iconic symbol of Dubai.

The Palm Jumeirah

-Explored Palm Jumeirah, an artificial archipelago in the Persian Gulf, known as one of Dubai's famous landmarks.

The Dubai Mariana canal

-Enjoyed a scenic cruise in Dubai Marina, a canal city along the Persian Gulf shoreline.

The Jumeirah Beach

-Witnessed a breathtaking sunset at Jumeirah Beach, offering a stunning view of natural beauty against Dubai's impressive skyline.
